# FlowForge - Company Profile

## Overview
FlowForge is a modern project management SaaS platform designed for small-to-medium teams (5–200 people). We help teams plan, track, and ship work faster with beautiful boards, automation, and AI-powered insights.

## Key Facts
- Founded: 2023
- Headquarters: Remote-first (Singapore & Karachi)
- Customers: 12,400+ active teams
- Pricing: Free (up to 10 users), Pro ($12/user/month), Enterprise (custom)
- Mission: Make project management feel effortless and delightful

## Core Values
- Speed & simplicity
- Radical transparency
- Customer obsession
- Continuous improvement

## Target Users
- Startup founders & product managers
- Marketing & design agencies
- Software development teams
- Freelancers & consultants
